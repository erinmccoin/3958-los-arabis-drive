The Property

Welcome to 3958 Los Arabis Drive, where timeless traditional design meets thoughtfully curated modern living. Nestled on a generous 0.84-acre lot in one of Lafayette's most coveted neighborhoods, this beautifully remodeled home spans over 2,600 square feet of refined living space. Classic architectural details are seamlessly woven together with contemporary finishes, creating a home that feels both warmly familiar and refreshingly current. From the moment you arrive, the property exudes curb appeal and quiet prestige — the kind of setting that's rare to find and even harder to leave.

Inside, this home reflects the care and vision of its remodel, offering updated spaces that are as functional as they are beautiful. The generous floor plan flows effortlessly for both everyday living and entertaining, while the expansive lot provides endless outdoor possibilities — whether you envision al fresco dining, lush gardens, or your own private retreat.

Located in the desirable Happy Valley, you'll enjoy top-rated Lafayette schools, easy access to Highway 24, BART, the beloved Lafayette Reservoir, scenic hiking and biking trails, and the charming shops and restaurants of downtown Lafayette. This is more than a home — it's a lifestyle. More photos and videos to come!
